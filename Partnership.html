Our Guiding Principles
& Story

Meritocracy Above All

We firmly believe in meritocracy. When the playing field is truly level, those who create the most value for society should rise to the top deservingly.

Humanity First, Beyond Borders

For us, inequality isn't confined to a single nation. It’s a global issue. We focus on reducing inequality between humans across all countries.

Empowerment Over Handouts

We don’t believe that charity or handouts can solve the deep-rooted problem of inequality. Real, sustainable change comes from empowerment.

The Beginning:

A Dream to Solve a Global Problem

In 2019, a group of friends—each of us first-generation immigrants from Africa—came together with a bold ambition: to tackle one of the world’s most daunting issues, economic inequality.

The problem: Economic Inequality

Having grown up at the lower end of the economic spectrum, we knew firsthand how deeply this problem affects lives.

The Vehicle: A Business with a Mission

We realized that the most impactful way to address inequality wasn’t through handouts, but through empowerment.

Our First Step: Evangadi Networks

Knowing the steps we took to benefit from the tech revolution, we realized that mentoring others would be a powerful start.

The Evolution:

From Mentorship to Full-Scale Training

Our "Five Step Week" methodology has been refined and perfected across numerous cohorts to accelerate students' journey to proficiency.

Our First Course: Overcoming Early Challenges

In November 2019, we launched our full-stack development course, starting from basic computer literacy and adapting it into the Amharic language.

Crafting a Transformative Teaching Methodology

Our "5-Step Week" methodology combines coding and soft skills through project-based learning, enabling beginners to build real-world applications.

The Curriculum

A 25-week intensive program that covers all aspects of full-stack application development.

Opening Doors for the Underserved

In May 2021, we launched a scholarship program to eliminate financial barriers for deserving students, awarding over 700 scholarships to individuals from disadvantaged backgrounds, but with over 10,000 applications, the demand far exceeds our current capacity, driving our need for additional partnerships and support.

700+ Full-Scholarships Awarded

Over 700 full scholarships awarded, empowering students from underserved communities.

3000+ Full Access Students

Over 3,000 students granted full access to our course materials, providing them with the opportunity to thrive.

10,000+ Applications so far

While we’re proud to have helped 700 students, over 10,000 others have already applied and are still waiting for their chance.
